    • About the Course

    Comm Resarch is a course that covers the most frequently used researchs methods found in communications. This course is lecture based while allowing for students to apply what they learn into real world experiences.

    About the Professor

    Dr. Haenschen, as the professor of this class, earned her bachelor's degree from Columbia University and her Ph.D from the University of Texas at Austin. With much experience in data driven research, Dr. Haenschen is the prime candidate for professor of Comm Research.
