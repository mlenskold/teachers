    • About the Course

    Introductory Sociology covers the basics of sociology as a general education course. It is open to all Sociology and Criminology majors, offering the department a hands-on teaching experience with students of the specific major.

    About the Professor

    Dr. Ryan is the Chair of the Sociology Department at Virginia Tech. A graduate of Vanderbilt Univeristy, Dr. Ryan has experience with research that has equipped him with all things necessary to properly teach this course.
