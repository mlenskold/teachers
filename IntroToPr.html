    • About the Course

    This course covers the basics of what it means to be in the Public Relations field. It goes over methods of communication, important figures in PR history, and much more. Structured as a hybrid class, half of the information is learned solely online while the other half is in the classroom.

    About the Professor

    Taught in class by Caitlin McDaniel, a graduate student at Virginia Tech, Caitlin follows all guidlines and procedudures given to her by Dr. Cannon, the course director. Dr Cannon had written an entire textbook and has also assisted in other publications. Earning his Ph.D at the University of Texas at Audtin, Dr. Cannon is a highly accredited public relations professor.
