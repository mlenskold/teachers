    • About the Course

    Visual Media is a communications based class taught by Dr. Carlos Evia. In this course, students are tasked with four main projects: magazine spread design, photographic composition, video producing, and website creation. All of these tasks were built to allow students to gain a solid understanding of softwares such as InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere, and Atom.

    About the Professor

    Dr. Evia has earned the following awards: Frank R. Smith Award for Distinguished Article, and Outstanding Faculty Outreach from Virginia Tech College of Liberal Arts and Human Sciences. Dr. Evia has also written numerous articles and performed a handful of research.
